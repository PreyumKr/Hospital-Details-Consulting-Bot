Cardiology

The Cardiology department is the Main site of healthcare of the hospital.

The department provides the best Magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) output. It provides the patients with Electrocardiogram (ECG) reports, it also provides Echocardiogram (ultrasound) reports of the heart on the go. The department provides the hospital the base for preparing for all other treatments.

Neurology

The Neurology department is the main site for all nerve problems.

The neurology departmenet treats diseases related to the brain and spinal cord, Peripheral nerves and muscles. They work on the diseases suchh as epilepsy, stroke, multiple sclerosis(MS), Parkinson's disease etc.

Hepatology

The Hepatology department is the main site for all liver problems

The Hepatology department works on diseases that effect liver, gallbladder, pancreas, billary tract etc.The department work on hepatic diseases such as hepatitis, fatty liver disease, pancreatitis and more.

Pediatrics

The pediatric department deals with disease for Younger children below age 18.

The pediatric department is the branch of medicine that invoves the medical care of infants, children and adolescents. pediatricians are trained to diagnose and treat a broad range of children illnesses.
